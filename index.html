The Platform

Every Layer of GridOps Intelligence's Asset Reliability Engine

GridOps Intelligence is a cloud-native SaaS platform built on a modular architecture real-time Edge-AI dashboards, legacy SCADA bridge integration, proprietary vectorization logic, and UK GDPR-compliant infrastructure designed to serve UK infrastructure SMEs without requiring internal data science teams.

Module 01

Multiaxial Asset Criticality Vectorization

Deep learning models that analyze real-time signals from meters, sensors, and SCADA logs evaluating each asset based on systemic impact, not just simple threshold breaches.

Calculates a "Dynamic Criticality Score" considering load variations, environmental stress, and grid position updating in real-time so operators prioritize high-risk components before cascading failures occur. Unlike generic IoT dashboards, this is asset-context-aware intelligence.
Module 02

Asset Behavioural Drift Detection Matrix

Identifies early-stage degradation through micro-variance accumulation analysis distinguishing between operational "noise" and genuine failure precursors with 94% accuracy.

Trained on UK-specific infrastructure asset profiles. Provides algorithmic identification of thermal, vibration, and electrical drifts that correlate with emerging failure signatures delivering a 14-day early-warning window unavailable to static alarm systems.
Module 03

Micro-Failure Event Correlation Synthesizer

Aggregates low-severity anomalies into composite failure signatures, providing a 14-day early-warning window for planned interventions across all asset classes.

Correlates disparate telemetry signals (thermal, vibration, and electrical) into a single unified failure prediction event. Traditional monitoring misses the "quiet inefficiencies" GridOps makes them visible, auditable, and actionable before they escalate.
Module 04

Edge-First Offline Monitoring Layer

Processes critical telemetry locally on NVIDIA Jetson modules ensuring 24/7 oversight for remote wind farms, rural substations, and low-connectivity industrial sites.

Sustains 95% of predictive functionality during complete network dropouts. Once reconnected, the cloud-integrated engine synchronizes data to update global reliability models. Localized processing also minimizes the data attack surface, enhancing cyber-physical security.
Module 05

Privacy-Preserving Federated Learning

Enables SMEs to benefit from industry-wide failure signatures through a federated repository without ever sharing raw, commercially sensitive operational data.

Creates a "Collective Resilience Moat" where every new asset deployment strengthens predictive accuracy for all users in the network. Fully aligned with UK GDPR and OT security standards. The only SME-focused tool solving the "small data" problem through encoded, privacy-first learning cycles.
Module 06

Automated NIS 2018 Compliance & Reporting

Automatically generates the audit trails and resilience reports required by Ofgem and UK regulators transforming compliance from an administrative burden into an operational advantage.

Maintains an immutable audit trail of maintenance actions and efficiency gains. Maps asset performance against UK energy standards and Net Zero compliance requirements in real-time making SMEs "audit-ready" without dedicated compliance teams or additional administrative overhead.

How It Works

From Fragmented Telemetry to Unified Reliability in Six Phases

GridOps Intelligence's predictive operations lifecycle moves UK infrastructure SMEs from reactive "fix-it-when-it-breaks" maintenance to proactive, data-driven reliability management protecting asset uptime, reducing grid costs, and ensuring permanent regulatory compliance.

Phase 01 · Connect

Connect & Ingest Legacy SCADA & Industrial Sensor Data

GridOps Intelligence connects directly to legacy SCADA systems, industrial IoT sensors, energy meters, and operational technology via a Zero-Overhaul software bridge. No hardware replacement. No middleware overhaul. The platform structures data from Modbus and DNP3 protocols instantly.

Apache Kafka and MQTT brokers automate the ingestion of high-frequency data from industrial sensors and legacy meters providing sub-300ms data pipeline latency across substations, renewable energy sites, and industrial facilities.

SCADA Bridge Setup Protocol-Agnostic APIs Real-Time Telemetry
Phase 02 · Vectorize

Build Each Asset's Unique Criticality Vector

The Multiaxial Asset Criticality Vectorization engine evaluates each asset based on its systemic impact calculating a "Criticality Score" that considers load variations, environmental stress, and grid position. Unlike generic threshold alerts, this is context-aware intelligence.

This Criticality Score is stored as the asset's permanent operational intelligence ensuring that failure patterns are recognized even through staff turnover or seasonal infrastructure changes, eliminating institutional "knowledge loss."

Criticality Scoring Asset Intelligence Systemic Impact Mapping
Phase 03 · Detect

Behavioral Drift Detection 14 Days Before Failure

The Asset Behavioural Drift Detection Matrix identifies "micro-variances" tiny anomalies in thermal, vibration, and electrical data that precede major failures. The system distinguishes genuine failure precursors from operational noise with 94% accuracy.

When a transformer begins subtle thermal drift or a wind turbine bearing shows irregular vibration patterns, GridOps detects the composite failure signature weeks before a catastrophic failure occurs providing a vital planned intervention window.

14-Day Early Warning Sub-500ms Alerts Drift Pattern Analysis
Phase 04 · Edge Process

Edge-AI Inference 24/7 Even Without Connectivity

The Edge-First architecture processes critical telemetry locally on NVIDIA Jetson Orin modules ensuring uninterrupted 24/7 monitoring for remote wind farms, rural substations, and industrial sites with limited or no network connectivity.

Unlike cloud-dependent competitors, GridOps sustains 95% of its predictive functionality during complete network dropouts. Localized processing also minimizes data attack surfaces providing built-in OT cybersecurity for infrastructure assets.

Offline-First AI 95% Uptime Offline OT Cyber Protection
Phase 05 · Act

Automated Maintenance Workflows & Prioritized Work Orders

GridOps automatically generates prioritized maintenance work orders based on failure probability and asset criticality scores. Front-line operators receive clear, actionable intelligence no data science team required. The right engineer is dispatched to the right asset at the right time.

Human-in-the-loop verification ensures high-risk criticality scores are reviewed by senior engineers before automated actions maintaining governance requirements for essential UK infrastructure services.

Auto Work Orders Criticality-Ranked Human-in-the-Loop
Phase 06 · Comply

Automated NIS 2018 & Ofgem Compliance Reporting

GridOps closes the loop automatically generating immutable audit trails and resilience reports required by Ofgem and UK NIS 2018 regulators. Compliance becomes an operational advantage, not a manual administrative burden.

Every maintenance action, criticality score, and efficiency gain is logged, encrypted, and stored in a GDPR-compliant digital archive keeping SME operators "audit-ready" at all times without dedicated compliance staff.

What types of UK infrastructure does GridOps Intelligence support?

+
GridOps Intelligence supports SME Independent Power Producers (IPPs), localized grid substations, renewable energy sites (solar and wind), water distribution networks, district heating networks, industrial manufacturing facilities, and facilities management estates. The platform's modular architecture allows rapid cross-sector deployment without retraining the core drift detection logic.

Do we need to replace our existing SCADA hardware?

+
No. GridOps Intelligence's proprietary "Zero-Overhaul" SCADA Bridge is specifically designed to extract and structure data from aging Modbus and DNP3 industrial protocols without requiring expensive hardware replacements. UK SMEs can unlock Tier-1 predictive intelligence from their existing infrastructure within weeks at 10% of the CAPEX of traditional enterprise upgrades.

How does the Federated Learning Repository protect our data?

+
GridOps encodes failure precursors into anonymized signatures using a federated learning architecture meaning raw, commercially sensitive operational data never leaves your infrastructure. The system allows SMEs to benefit from industry-wide failure patterns without sharing sensitive asset data. All data is processed under UK GDPR, stored in Microsoft Azure UK South data centres, and aligned with ISO/IEC 27001 and OT security standards.

Does GridOps work in remote sites without internet connectivity?

+
Yes. GridOps is built with an "Offline-First" Edge-AI architecture. Critical telemetry is processed locally on NVIDIA Jetson Orin modules, ensuring 24/7 monitoring even in remote wind farms and rural substations with limited or no connectivity. The system sustains 95% of its predictive functionality during complete network dropouts, and synchronizes data once reconnected.

Full GridOps predictive operations deployment is typically operational within 4–6 weeks. Weeks 1–2 cover SCADA bridge connection and legacy protocol integration. Weeks 2–3 involve asset criticality baseline establishment and drift detection calibration. Weeks 3–5 see live failure precursor detection and alert validation. Why can competitors not easily replicate GridOps Intelligence?

GridOps' competitive moat is built on five defensible pillars: (1) the proprietary Zero-Overhaul SCADA bridge requiring deep legacy protocol domain expertise; (2) the federated failure precursor repository that grows stronger with every deployment; (3) native UK NIS 2018 and Ofgem compliance alignment that global giants ignore; (4) the hardware-software synergy of lightweight Edge-AI that makes enterprise rip-and-replace obsolete; and (5) UK-specific asset training datasets unavailable to generic IIoT platforms.
